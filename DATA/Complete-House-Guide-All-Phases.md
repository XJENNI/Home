## Concrete Mix Ratios — **USE M20 MINIMUM FOR ALL RCC**

| Grade | Ratio | Water:Cement | Use in Your House | Strength |
|-------|-------|--------------|-------------------|----------|
| M15 | 1:2:4 | 0.50 | PCC bed below foundation only | 15 N/mm² |
| **M20 (MINIMUM)** | **1:1.5:3** | **0.45** | **Foundation, columns, beams, ALL slabs** | **20 N/mm²** |
| M25 | 1:1:2 | 0.40 | Critical structural if engineer specifies | 25 N/mm² |
| Mortar 1:6 | — | — | Brickwork masonry IS 2250 | — |
| Plaster 1:4 | — | — | Internal + external plaster IS 2250 | — |

**CURING RULE:** Wet-cure all concrete minimum 14 days (28 days for roof slab). Bihar summer dries concrete too fast — cover with wet jute + water 3x/day.

## Key Decisions

✅ **Fe500D Steel** (not Fe500) — high ductility for Bihar Seismic Zone III  
✅ **M25 for footings and roof slab** — extra strength for long-term durability  
✅ **M20 for beams/columns** — acceptable standard, cost-effective  
❌ **Never use Fe415 bars** even if contractor suggests it
