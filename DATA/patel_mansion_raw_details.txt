PHASE 1: FOUNDATION & STRUCTURE (EXCAVATION TO ROOF)
Duration: 10 weeks (Weeks 2-13) | Cost: I27,45,000
SCOPE OF WORK
• Excavation: Dig 30 pits (5 ft deep × 4×4 ft each) for isolated footings
• PCC Bed: M15 plain cement concrete 150mm thick below all footings (anti-soil contact)
• RCC Footings: M25 concrete, Fe500D steel, 30 footings (1200×1200×400mm each)
• Plinth Beam: 230×380mm RCC M20 connecting all 30 footings at ground level
• Columns: 30 nos, 230×300mm, 20 ft height (GF + FF), Fe500D 12mm + 8mm bars
• GF Beams: 230×460mm RCC M20 at 10 ft height on all column tops
• GF Slab: 125mm thick M25 RCC slab (first floor base) + 28-day curing
• FF Beams: 230×460mm RCC M20 at 20 ft height (roof level)
• Roof Slab: 125mm thick M25 RCC slab with waterproofing slope + 28-day curing
• Seismic Compliance: Bihar Zone III – ductile detailing, stirrups @ 100mm spacing

- Wipe bathroom LED mirrors (anti-fog coating)
- Fan remote batteries check
- Camera lens clean
- Smart lock test all methods

- Test RCCB trip function (press test button)
- Inspect outdoor lights for water ingress
- Tighten MCB terminals if loose
- Solar panel wash
- Window track lubrication
- Audio zone test

## Monsoon Season (June-September — 2 hours DIY)
- Window seals inspection
- Camera housing check
- Smart lock battery replacement
- Door seals verification
- Check roof drainage near conduit entries
- Test RCCB more frequently (moisture increases leakage risk)

## Annual (Professional — ₹4,600)
- Earthing system test (<5 ohms professional check)
- Insulation resistance megger test
- Fan bearing lubrication (if not sealed)
- Bathroom mirror touch sensor calibration
- Solar professional service
- NVR HDD check

## Every 5 Years (₹80K-1.2L)
- Exterior repaint + waterproofing touch-up
- Tile re-grouting
- Major system inspection
