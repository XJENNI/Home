Walkthrough Mode — First Person View

Click Enable Walkthrough, then click inside the viewport to lock controls. Use WASD or arrow keys to move, mouse to look around. Press Esc to exit.

AR Mode — Place in Your Space

On a supported mobile device, tap Start AR to place the scaled Patel Mansion model in your real environment. The model rotates so you can inspect it from all angles.

AR requires WebXR support. If the AR button is greyed out, your browser/device does not support it — use the walkthrough above instead.
